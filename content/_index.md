Welcome to my website—my digital space! My name is Chisel Li, and I am currently a junior majoring in Journalism at Lanzhou University. I am dedicated to becoming a social science researcher who uses computational methods to describe, infer, and ultimately discover the world. My current research and academic interests focus on communication issues related to visual modalities, political communication, social media and society, as well as corresponding computational communication methodologies. I hope to use this online space to connect with others who share these interests.
