Welcome to my website-my back stage! My name is Chisel Li, and I am currently a junior majoring in Journalism at Lanzhou University in China. I am dedicated to becoming a social science researcher who uses computational methods to describe, infer, and ultimately discover the world. My current research and academic interests focus on visual culture and communication, political communication, social media and society, as well as corresponding computational communication methodologies. I hope to use this online space to connect with others who share these interests.
